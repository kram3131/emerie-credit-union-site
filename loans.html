The Mortgage Process

Prequalification

Online or by phone, no credit impact

Application

Full application with tax returns, bank statements, pay stubs

Processing

Appraisal, employment verification, file review

Conditional Approval

Underwriter reviews; remaining conditions identified

Clear to Close

All conditions met; Closing Disclosure issued

Closing

Sign documents at title company; funds disbursed

Typical timeline: 30 to 45 days from application to closing.

Refinance Options

Rate-and-Term Refinance

Lower your payment, shorten your term, or switch from adjustable to fixed. Same rates as purchase loans.

Cash-Out Refinance

Max 80% LTV (conventional), 85% (FHA), 90% (VA). Rates typically 0.125% to 0.250% above standard.

Streamline Refinance

FHA Streamline and VA IRRRL with reduced documentation. May not require a new appraisal.
