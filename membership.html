Member Accounts

Checking, share savings, and share certificates designed for every stage of life. All deposits federally insured by NCUA up to $250,000.

Membership Eligibility

To open any account at Emerie Credit Union, you must first qualify for membership. Membership is open to anyone who lives, works, worships, or attends school in Williamson, Travis, Bell, or Hays County, Texas, and to immediate family members of existing members.

A $5.00 deposit into a Primary Share Savings Account establishes your membership and your ownership stake in the credit union. This account must remain open and funded as long as you are a member.

Overdraft Options

All accounts default to no overdraft coverage.

Overdraft Transfer Protection

Link to a share savings account or line of credit. Funds transfer in $100 increments. $5.00 per transfer.

Standard Overdraft Coverage

Credit union may authorize overdrafts on checks and recurring debits. $29.00 per item, max 3 per day ($87.00 daily cap). Items $5.00 or less exempt.

No Overdraft Coverage

Transactions that would overdraw the account are declined at no charge. This is the default for all new accounts.
