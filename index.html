Rooted in Central Texas

Founded in Georgetown in 1987, Emerie Credit Union is member-owned and governed by a volunteer board of directors made up of Central Texas community leaders.

As a not-for-profit cooperative, our earnings go back to members in the form of better rates and lower fees, not to outside shareholders.
