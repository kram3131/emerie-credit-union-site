Our Story

Nearly four decades of member-first financial services, rooted in the heart of Central Texas.

Emerie Credit Union exists to improve the financial well-being of our members and strengthen the communities we serve. As a member-owned cooperative, every decision we make is guided by one question: is this good for our members?

Member Ownership

You are not a customer here. You are a member-owner. Every person who opens an account at Emerie Credit Union becomes an equal owner of the institution, with voting rights and a share of our success.

Local Decision-Making

Your loan applications, financial needs, and life goals are reviewed right here in Central Texas by people who understand the local economy and care about its growth.

Community First

With more than $300,000 in annual giving and 16 hours of paid volunteer time for every team member, investing in Central Texas is part of who we are.

Rooted in Georgetown Since 1987

Emerie Credit Union opened its doors in Georgetown, Texas in 1987, chartered as a member-owned cooperative to serve families and workers who believed banking should benefit the people, not just the institution. At a time when large banks were consolidating and moving decision-making farther from the communities they served, our founders chose a different path.

Nearly four decades later, that founding belief drives everything we do. From our original Georgetown headquarters, we have grown to five branches across Central Texas, serving members in Round Rock, Cedar Park, the Austin Domain area, and Temple.

Today, Emerie Credit Union operates under a Texas state credit union charter, regulated by the Texas Credit Union Department and insured by the National Credit Union Administration (NCUA). Governance remains in the hands of our members, through a volunteer board of directors elected by and from the membership.
